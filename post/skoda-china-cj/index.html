SKODA CHINA customer journey

· 2019-08-28 · # Design Thinking # Research # Designing Life

In order to improve auto sales decline and low brand recognition in China, VW SKODA delegated Accenture to explore and optimize the experience of China Customer Journey.

Starting from customer-centric service, we defined several typical architypes via in-depth interview and research, and depicted corresponding customer journey AS-IS to reflect their experience, needs and expectations.

In addition, we also organized an innovation workshop that involved stakeholders from clients and customers, to jointly create the upgraded products and service offerings, then successfully envisioned China Customer Journey To-Bes. Tangible items delivered to VW SKODA include a backlog of service offerings and concepts, Customer Journey As-Ises and To-Bes for four architypes, and a project report.

In Accenture, we adhere to the value proposition of our clients, help Global Brands better understand their customers in local markets, enhance the brand loyalty, and facilitate digital transformation and experience optimization.
