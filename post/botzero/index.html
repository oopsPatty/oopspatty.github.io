BotZero - AI Chatbot Platform for Enterprises

· 2019-06-13 · # Artificial Intelligence # Product # Patty's Design


Unlike the traditional chatbot platforms, which prevalently require huge coding workload to develop, and the worse is, are unable to understand diverse and complicated dialogs, BotZero's constant reinforcement learning skills easily carry multi-round conversation and assist users to complete complex task. Most important is, this virtual assistant factory only requires to input dialogs with zero code to build agents.


# My adventure begins here BotZero is the first project after I joined Accenture SH, also my first adventure roaming in the realm of Artificial Intelligence. When I started to get my hands dirty, a rough and basic product frame already done with technical accessibility other than user experience. Thus, my first year with BotZero is all about building a greater agent curator and leveraging its usability and efficiency. Let's see how my journey went.
